Welcome to The Last Word Bookshop!

The Last Word is an independant bookshop with a collection of both used and brand new books. Our staff are more than happy to help you find whatever it is you're looking for (and perhaps even a few things you didn't know you were looking for!). We have an expansive collection of fantasy and sci fi (the owner's favorite genres!), from reknowned stories to hidden gems. Here you can find something for everyone.
